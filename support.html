How to join a game

  • Open an invite notification or a shared game link.
  • Enter your buy-in to join.
  • If you’re already active in another game, FP will ask you to finish or leave it first.

How settlements work (high level)

  • During the game, FP tracks buy-ins and cash-outs.
  • When the host ends the game, all active players enter their final stack amount.
  • FP calculates who should pay whom to settle up, while minimizing friction and keeping transfers consistent.
  • FP does not process payments. Settlements are guidance for your group to settle privately.

Common issues

I can’t join (expired / full / kicked)

FP blocks joins when the link is expired, the table is full, or the host removed you from the game.

Notifications

  • If you aren’t receiving invites or game-ended alerts, check notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Some dealer-round reminders may be delayed by the operating system’s battery optimizations.

A number was entered wrong

  • During live play, the host can correct totals (with an Activity entry for transparency).
  • During end-game totals collection, you may be able to edit your submitted total until finalization starts.
  • If needed, the host can trigger a recount so players re-enter totals.

Report a bug

Please include:

  • Your device (iPhone / Android model)
  • FP version/build (from Settings → App Info)
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or screen recording (if possible)
